2. Signs of a stroke

The F.A.S.T. test is an easy way to remember the most common signs of stroke. Face. Check their face. Has their mouth drooped? Arms. Can they lift both arms? Speech. Is their speech slurred? Do they understand you? Time is critical. If you see any of these signs call triple zero (000) straight away.
